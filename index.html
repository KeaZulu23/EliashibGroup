About Us

Eliashib Group of Companies (hereinafter referred to as “Eliashib Group”) started from humble yet fulfilling beginnings, it has gone through the development over the years, and now it is one of the diversified investment groups in South Africa.

  • It built up an innovative business model of “strategic investments + financial investments”.
  • Through strategic investments, the Company invests in companies across different segments.
  • IT, financial services, modern services, agriculture and food, property,mining, renewable energy, infrastructure and construction, manufacturing, media and hospitality.

The Company has concluded its distinctive investment concepts and management system based on the deep understanding of economies and enterprises. Through forward-looking layout, flexible investment strategies and sustained value-added services, Eliashib Group has cultivated a number of influential outstanding enterprises in several sectors.

Strategic Investment

Through strategic investments business, Eliashib Group focuses on strategic directions with great potentials, and invests and manages the portfolio sustainably for long-term returns. As a corporation rising from industries and knowing laws of corporate development and management well, Eliashib Group offers specific management improvements, brand endorsements, financial support and some other value-added services to its invested companies, and helps its member companies develop rapidly and realize the leap from outstanding to excellent.

Financial Investment

Through financial investments businesses focusing on angel investment, venture capital investment, and private equity investment, Eliashib Group has built a complete investment industrial chain across all stages of a company's life cycle, discovering investment opportunities in each development stage of a company, helping its invested companies to realize value growth, bringing healthy returns for investors, and constantly reserving and providing project resources for Eliashib Group's strategic investments businesses.

Technology

Our key focus is in FinTech, AgriTech, HealthTech and other cutting-edge technologies with high impact.

Building & Construction

African is in a reconstruction mode both culturally and structurally, we therefore aim to have a dent in the residential and commercial spaces through infrastructure development projects.

Health

Through tech innovations we envisage to help health sectors around the continent to improve service delivery and save lives.

Energy & Gas

The African continent is facing power shortages and global warming challenges, though the continent is also full of opportunities through its natural resources. Eliashib Group and its strategic partners embarked on energy projects to bring solutions for cleaner, efficient and affordable energy.

Manufacturing

For decades raw materials have been shipped out of African countries and sold back as finished products. Through, our focus in manufacturing we aim to set up beneficiation plants and manufacturing plants employing local skills.

Media

Through the advent of technology and social media, media has become one of the powerful tools to shape society through education, entertainment and various forms. Adding this portfolio into our stable will help Africans to tell their own stories, their own way.

Food and Beverages

In collaboration with our manufacturing and agricultural wings we aim to enter the global market by exporting quality locally produced food and beverages.

Hospitality

Entering this sector with innovative and sustainable plans will ensure preservation of our biodiversity and cultural awareness, while adding value to our economies.

Agriculture

Our focus is in farming and agri-processing to increase food production and security.

Mining

We employ our expertise in exploration, mining, beneficiation and marketing of commodities such as chrome, manganese, Rare Earth Elements, coal, Iron Ore and others.

Our Vision

Eliashib Group is committed to becoming a respected and trusted global holding company, with equity in leading international enterprises.
